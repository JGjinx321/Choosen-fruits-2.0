Is there a return policy?

Due to the nature of fruits and their nature to sometimes have short ripe periods, there is no return policy for fruits themselves.
However, if you receive a damaged item, please contact us within 48 hours of receiving your order. We will work with you to resolve the issue as quickly as possible.
Please include your order number and a description of the issue in your email. We may request photos of the damaged item to assist with the resolution process.
Once we receive your email, we will review the information and get back to you within 2 business days with a solution.
Solutions may include a replacement item, a refund, or store credit, depending on the circumstances.
We appreciate your understanding and cooperation in this matter. Our goal is to ensure that you are satisfied with your purchase and our service.
